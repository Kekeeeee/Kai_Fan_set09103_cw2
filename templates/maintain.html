Why should we take care of our car?

The car is just like our child

A car is made up of thousands of parts. The performance of functional components decreases with wear, aging, corrosion, etc. This kind of change happens gradually in many parts when the vehicle is moving normally. No vehicle is used in exactly the same way, and we cannot predict the wear and tear of each component under inspection. Therefore, it is necessary to check and maintain the parts regularly, so that the performance of the vehicle is restored to the best condition.

Especially at front of the vehicle brake, direction, and the technical performance of the tire, lights, horn, the rearview mirror is complete and effective, all kinds of oil, coolant, brake fluid to maintain normal, etc. These are all drivers should check before every car, don't be careless, lightly, once broke down on the road, will bring needless trouble to oneself, one thousand had an accident, the consequences can not imagine.
